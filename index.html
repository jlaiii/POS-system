ℹ️ How it works

When the total item quantity in the cart reaches or exceeds the Party Size Threshold, a service charge line is automatically added to the cart at the configured Percentage of the subtotal. The service charge is displayed on the cart, receipt, kiosk, and order history. It applies per-order when the condition is met.
